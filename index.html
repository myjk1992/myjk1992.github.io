Hello! I am a Ph.D. student at Computer Vision Lab at Kyoto University under the supervision of Prof. Shohei Nobuhara and Prof. Ko Nishino. My research interests include: computer vision, 3D capture in water, computational photography, structure-from-motion, projector-camera systems, multiple view geometry, and (self-)calibration.
